About Me!

My name is Hafeez Abdallah. I am a Digital Marketing Strategist and Web Developer based in Abuja, Nigeria. I have been helping businesses succeed online for over 5 years.

I am the founder of InfoSuleja - Facts About Suleja; a platform with presence on both Facebook and Instagram which goal is to put the city of Suleja on the digital map. With over 5,000 followers on Facebook, I can happily call that a success.

I started designing websites on my mobile device using XML on the now defunct Wapka.mobi and Peperonity.com around 2008. I then came across Wordpress and Blogger as far back as 2012. I still run my Wordpress blog but not actively.
